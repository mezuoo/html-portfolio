The Best Movies of All Time According to Zuo

My Top X Movies of All time

Gran Turismo

As a car geek, the experience of watching this movie for the first time is incomparable to any other thing. Extremely adrenaline-inducing, great casting, great direction, an overall masterpiece.

Fast Five

Another car related movie, easily my preferred of the Fast & Furious franchise.

Se7en

As a lover of psychologically tasking cinema, this movie is undoubtedly one of the most satisfying movies I've ever watched. Amazing storyline to begin with, it maintaons the element of suspense, keeping the viewer hooked all through the watch.
